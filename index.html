Litu Rout

I am a Scientist at Space Applications Centre (SAC), Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). I received my bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) from the Department of Avionics, Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST) in July 2018. During my Bachelor's program, I was advised by Rama Krishna Sai Subrahmanyam Gorthi and Deepak Mishra.

At SAC, I develop machine/deep learning based operational solutions as a part of the Optical Data Processing Division, Signal and Image Processing Group.

Research

I study AI at the intersection of Computer Vision, Adversarial Learning and Reinforcement Learning including the theoretical aspects of sample complexity and generalization puzzles. I primarily focus my research on building artificially intelligent machines that perceive the world from sensory data the way humans do. Following is a list of selected publications.

WAEF: Weighted Aggregation with Enhancement Filter for Visual Object Tracking
Litu Rout, Deepak Mishra and Rama Krishna Gorthi
ECCV Workshop, 2018
PDF

This paper discusses a novel approach to regress in temporal domain, based on weighted aggregation of distinctive visual features and feature prioritization with entropy estimation in a recursive fashion.

WACV, 2018

A Method for Sequential Information Condensation using Fourier Basis
Tapan Misra, Litu Rout
SAC, ISRO, 2020
Application No. 202041004166

The present embodiment proposes an efficient Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) based hyper-spectral image compression technique to store multiple acquisitions over same region of interest and thereby, improve Signal to Noise Ratio (SNR) of hyper-spectral images which usually have coarse spatial resolution.
