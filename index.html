Litu Rout

I am a Scientist at Space Applications Centre (SAC), Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). I received my bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) from the Department of Avionics, Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST) in July 2018. During my Bachelor's program, I was advised by Rama Krishna Sai Subrahmanyam Gorthi and Deepak Mishra.

At SAC, I develop machine/deep learning based operational solutions as a part of the Optical Data Processing Division, Signal and Image Processing group.

Research

I study AI at the intersection of Computer Vision, Adversarial Learning and Reinforcement Learning including the theoretical aspects of sample complexity and generalization puzzles. I primarily focus my research on building artificially intelligent machines that perceive the world from sensory data the way humans do. Representative papers are highlighted.
